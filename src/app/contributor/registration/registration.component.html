NowCE Position on ‘Disclosure’

If you are preparing material for users seeking certification from a board accredited education provider, it is generally required that all contributors make a declaration about any financial or other interests they might have which are associated with the speciality that is the focus of the course content.

NowCE supports the level of compliance [consistent with the AACME requirement]. If you have no interests to disclose please use the next form to say this. -No course will be published unless the author and other contributors disclose their related interests or confirm they have none. Failure to do this might invalidate the authentication for the student and infringe upon accreditation regulations. And there is a benefit for you too.

After you have completed this step we will provide you with a URL for you to pass to other assignees so they can upload your disclosure details from a single location.

As an option - add your credentials and citations to enjoy the appropriate recognition and the convenience of a single referral source that is always under your control.
